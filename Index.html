The Formulas

For reference
MRP
MRP = Cost Price ÷ (1 − Margin %)
Gross Profit
Profit = Selling Price − Cost Price
Discount Percentage
Discount % = Discount Value ÷ MRP × 100
Discount Value
Discount Value = MRP × Discount %
Final Selling Price
Final Price = MRP − Discount Value
Margin Percentage
Margin % = (Selling Price − Cost Price) ÷ Selling Price × 100
